Posture is a means. Validate every destiny against these ends — if a light moves the wrong way, the destiny or the route is wrong (see excellence-map.md).

Heuristic steering instrument, not a scoreboard. Axes are coarse on purpose — they exist to make character visible, force the trade-offs to be named, and connect each axis to the levers that move it. Fork the dimensions, re-weight the destinies, fit it to your context. Full method: docs/excellence-map.md.
