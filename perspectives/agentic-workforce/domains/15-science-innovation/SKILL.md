# Science, Research, Standards, and Innovation Workforce

## Mission

Discover truth, invent capabilities, validate claims, and turn knowledge into useful systems.

## Core Jobs

- Design experiments and build evidence.
- Replicate, peer review, publish, patent, standardize, and commercialize discoveries.
- Maintain standards, metrology, labs, and reference systems.
- Govern ethics and dual-use risk.
- Translate research into products, policy, and industrial capability.

## Human Role Families

- Research scientist, principal investigator, lab manager, research associate.
- Data scientist, computational scientist, statistician, bioinformatician.
- Lab technician, instrumentation specialist, metrologist.
- Grant writer, research administrator, technology transfer officer.
- Patent attorney, standards engineer, regulatory scientist.
- AI researcher, robotics researcher, human factors researcher.

## AI Personnel

- Literature review agent: find, compare, and cite relevant evidence.
- Hypothesis generator: propose mechanisms and experiments.
- Experiment planner: draft protocols, controls, and measurement plans.
- Simulation agent: test designs virtually before lab execution.
- Lab data analyst: clean, model, and visualize results.
- Patent landscape analyst: map prior art and freedom to operate.
- Reproducibility checker: identify missing methods, data, and controls.

## Humanoid Robot Roles

- Lab automation, sample handling, equipment loading.
- Hazardous-material support.
- Field research logistics and repetitive measurement support.

## Accountability Boundary

Keep research ethics, publication claims, intellectual property strategy, animal/human subject decisions, dual-use release decisions, and standards approval human-governed.

## Agent Context Requirements

Load literature, protocols, lab safety rules, data dictionaries, instrumentation manuals, grant requirements, IRB/IACUC constraints, IP strategy, and reproducibility criteria.
