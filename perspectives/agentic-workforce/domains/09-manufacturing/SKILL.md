# Manufacturing and Industrial Production Workforce

## Mission

Convert designs and materials into reliable goods at scale.

## Core Jobs

- Design, source, produce, inspect, package, and ship goods.
- Detect quality drift and correct process.
- Replan production as demand changes.
- Maintain machinery and restore uptime.
- Automate safely and improve productivity.

## Human Role Families

- Manufacturing engineer, process engineer, industrial engineer.
- Production supervisor, plant manager, operations manager.
- Machinist, CNC programmer, welder, assembler, fabricator.
- Quality assurance manager, quality control inspector, metrologist.
- Maintenance technician, reliability engineer, controls engineer.
- Robotics engineer, automation engineer, PLC technician, mechatronics technician.

## AI Personnel

- Production scheduler: optimize work orders, labor, machines, and materials.
- Quality anomaly detector: flag defects, drift, and root-cause candidates.
- CAD/CAM assistant: generate toolpaths, fixtures, and design-for-manufacturing checks.
- Supplier risk agent: monitor lead time, defects, sanctions, and resilience.
- Work-instruction generator: produce operator guidance and training.
- Digital twin simulation agent: test process changes before implementation.

## Humanoid Robot Roles

- Assembly, kitting, material movement, inspection, rework support.
- Machine tending and tool changing.
- Brownfield factory support where spaces are human-designed.

## Accountability Boundary

Keep safety lockout, final quality release, labor relations, hazardous process authorization, automation safety certification, and plant leadership human-accountable.

## Agent Context Requirements

Load BOMs, routings, work instructions, quality specs, machine data, safety procedures, labor rules, supplier data, and maintenance history.
