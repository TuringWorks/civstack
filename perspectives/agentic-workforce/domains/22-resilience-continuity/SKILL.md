# Resilience, Continuity, and Strategic Foresight Workforce

## Mission

Keep the country functioning through shocks and long-range change.

## Core Jobs

- Identify weak signals and prepare before failure.
- Maintain continuity of government, food, water, energy, health, finance, communications, and logistics.
- Coordinate claims, rebuilding, mental health, supply chains, and accountability after shocks.
- Stress-test systems and invest in options across future scenarios.

## Human Role Families

- Enterprise risk manager, business continuity manager, emergency planner.
- National security planner, infrastructure resilience analyst, scenario planner.
- Supply chain risk manager, insurance catastrophe modeler.
- Crisis communications lead, recovery program manager, mutual aid coordinator.

## AI Personnel

- Scenario generation agent: produce plausible futures and stress tests.
- Dependency mapping agent: map infrastructure, suppliers, institutions, and failure cascades.
- Crisis dashboard analyst: summarize live signals and resource status.
- Continuity plan reviewer: test plans for gaps and assumptions.
- Supply disruption monitor: track bottlenecks, alternatives, and lead times.
- Claims triage agent: classify damage, evidence, and recovery pathways.

## Humanoid Robot Roles

- Emergency warehousing and shelter operations.
- Debris assessment and field logistics.
- Hazardous support and supply delivery.

## Accountability Boundary

Keep political prioritization, emergency powers, scarce resource allocation, evacuation orders, recovery justice, and continuity-of-government authority human-legitimate.

## Agent Context Requirements

Load risk registers, continuity plans, dependency maps, emergency authorities, inventories, mutual aid agreements, resource priorities, communications plans, and recovery criteria.
