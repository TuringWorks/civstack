# Transportation, Logistics, Postal, and Mobility Workforce

## Mission

Move people and goods through networks safely, predictably, and economically.

## Core Jobs

- Plan routes, consolidate loads, operate hubs, clear customs, and deliver.
- Provide safe roads, transit, aviation, rail, maritime, and pedestrian systems.
- Reroute and communicate during disruptions.
- Inspect, maintain, and upgrade transport infrastructure.
- Coordinate import/export movement and last-mile distribution.

## Human Role Families

- Truck driver, delivery driver, courier, bus operator, train operator.
- Pilot, air traffic controller, flight dispatcher, aircraft mechanic.
- Port operator, longshore worker, customs broker, freight forwarder.
- Logistics coordinator, supply chain manager, warehouse manager.
- Traffic engineer, transit planner, fleet manager, route optimization analyst.
- Postal carrier, mail processing clerk, last-mile operations manager.

## AI Personnel

- Routing optimizer: plan multimodal movements with cost, speed, and risk.
- Customs documentation agent: prepare and validate forms, classifications, and evidence.
- Inventory positioning agent: place goods near demand.
- Fleet maintenance predictor: schedule repairs from telemetry and usage.
- Disruption-response coordinator: reroute around weather, labor, conflict, and outages.
- Customer delivery communications agent: update status and exceptions.

## Humanoid Robot Roles

- Warehouse picking, packing, sorting, loading support.
- Mail sorting and last-100-feet delivery assistance.
- Station/airport guidance and maintenance inspection support.
- Port and cross-dock material movement under safety controls.

## Accountability Boundary

Keep safety-critical vehicle operation, air traffic control authority, hazardous goods approval, customs enforcement, labor safety, and public transport policy human-accountable.

## Agent Context Requirements

Load routes, manifests, bills of lading, customs rules, tariff classifications, fleet telemetry, warehouse maps, transport safety rules, and disruption feeds.
