# Finance, Insurance, Payments, and Capital Markets Workforce

## Mission

Move money, price risk, allocate capital, protect savings, and enable commerce.

## Core Jobs

- Move money reliably and prevent fraud.
- Assess risk and allocate funds.
- Insure, hedge, reserve, and regulate uncertainty.
- Account, audit, report, and comply.
- Advise consumers and institutions within fiduciary and suitability boundaries.

## Human Role Families

- Banker, loan officer, credit analyst, underwriter.
- Accountant, auditor, controller, financial reporting manager.
- Actuary, risk analyst, compliance analyst, model risk manager.
- Trader, portfolio manager, investment analyst, wealth advisor.
- Claims adjuster, insurance agent, fraud investigator.
- Payments operations analyst, AML analyst, sanctions analyst.
- Fintech product manager, quant researcher, AI risk lead.

## AI Personnel

- KYC/AML review agent: prepare customer and transaction risk packets.
- Fraud detection agent: detect anomalies and escalation patterns.
- Credit memo drafter: summarize borrower, collateral, cash flow, and risks.
- Portfolio research agent: gather market, company, macro, and risk evidence.
- Claims triage agent: classify severity, evidence, coverage, and next steps.
- Reconciliation agent: match ledgers, statements, trades, and exceptions.
- Regulatory reporting assistant: draft reports and evidence trails.

## Humanoid Robot Roles

- Branch concierge and secure document handling.
- Back-office logistics and facilities support.

## Accountability Boundary

Keep credit denial, fiduciary advice, claims disputes, market conduct, financial crime escalation, audit signoff, and systemic risk decisions human-accountable.

## Agent Context Requirements

Load product rules, customer permissions, risk models, audit standards, regulations, market data, transaction history, sanctions lists, suitability rules, and escalation thresholds.
