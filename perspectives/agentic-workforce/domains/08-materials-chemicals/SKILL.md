# Mining, Materials, Chemicals, and Industrial Inputs Workforce

## Mission

Extract and transform raw materials into safe, reliable inputs for the economy.

## Core Jobs

- Locate, extract, process, refine, transport, and certify materials.
- Monitor hazardous processes and environmental compliance.
- Diversify supply and recycle critical materials.
- Investigate material defects and improve specifications.
- Maintain process safety and worker protection.

## Human Role Families

- Mining engineer, geologist, equipment operator, mine safety manager.
- Chemical engineer, process engineer, plant operator, refinery technician.
- Metallurgist, materials scientist, quality engineer, lab technician.
- Environmental health and safety manager, hazardous materials specialist.
- Supply chain analyst, critical minerals strategist, recycling operations manager.

## AI Personnel

- Exploration data analyst: interpret geologic, seismic, geochemical, and remote data.
- Process optimization agent: recommend setpoints and maintenance changes.
- Safety incident predictor: detect precursor patterns.
- Chemical literature agent: synthesize reactions, hazards, and alternatives.
- Materials discovery agent: search compositions and properties.
- Compliance agent: prepare permit, reporting, and inspection evidence.

## Humanoid Robot Roles

- Hazardous inspection and sample handling.
- Lab and plant logistics.
- Maintenance support in controlled areas.
- Disaster inspection where human entry is dangerous.

## Accountability Boundary

Keep mine safety, hazardous release response, environmental permits, community consent, shutdown decisions, and certification human-led.

## Agent Context Requirements

Load SDS sheets, process safety information, permits, geologic data, quality specs, sampling plans, incident history, and emergency procedures.
