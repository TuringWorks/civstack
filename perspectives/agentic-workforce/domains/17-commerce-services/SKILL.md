# Commerce, Retail, Hospitality, and Customer Operations Workforce

## Mission

Match demand to goods and services, create satisfying experiences, and keep commercial operations profitable.

## Core Jobs

- Discover demand, stock inventory, price, sell, fulfill, support, and retain.
- Understand customer intent and resolve issues.
- Coordinate labor, space, safety, and experience for in-person services.
- Adapt offerings and channels as markets change.

## Human Role Families

- Retail associate, store manager, merchandiser, buyer.
- Account executive, sales development representative, customer success manager.
- Customer support specialist, contact center manager, support operations analyst.
- Hotel front desk manager, housekeeper, concierge, event manager.
- Restaurant manager, chef, line cook, server, food service worker.
- E-commerce manager, marketplace operations manager, growth marketer.

## AI Personnel

- Sales research agent: identify accounts, stakeholders, triggers, and needs.
- Proposal generator: draft quotes, scopes, and business cases.
- Customer support agent: resolve routine requests and route exceptions.
- Retention analyst: detect churn risk and recommend interventions.
- Inventory planning agent: forecast demand and replenishment.
- Pricing analyst: test elasticity, margin, competition, and inventory effects.
- Marketing campaign agent: draft segments, messages, and experiments.

## Humanoid Robot Roles

- Shelf stocking and retail floor retrieval.
- Room service delivery and housekeeping support.
- Bussing tables, dish handling, and event setup.
- Queue assistance and wayfinding.

## Accountability Boundary

Keep brand trust, customer recovery, labor management, regulated sales, safety incidents, high-value negotiation, and final pricing strategy human-led.

## Agent Context Requirements

Load product catalog, inventory, pricing rules, customer history, service policies, safety procedures, brand voice, labor schedule, and escalation rules.
