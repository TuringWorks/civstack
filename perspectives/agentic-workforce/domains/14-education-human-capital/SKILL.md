# Education, Training, Libraries, and Human Capital Workforce

## Mission

Form capable people, transmit knowledge, cultivate judgment, and reskill the workforce.

## Core Jobs

- Teach literacy, numeracy, science, citizenship, collaboration, and self-regulation.
- Assess skills and train workers efficiently.
- Preserve, classify, retrieve, and teach knowledge.
- Adapt instruction for struggling learners.
- Assess competence fairly and credential capability.

## Human Role Families

- Teacher, professor, teaching assistant, tutor, instructional coach.
- Curriculum designer, learning experience designer, assessment specialist.
- School counselor, special education teacher, speech-language pathologist.
- Librarian, archivist, museum educator, knowledge manager.
- Corporate trainer, workforce development specialist, apprenticeship coordinator.
- Education administrator, registrar, student success manager.

## AI Personnel

- Tutor agent: diagnose gaps and adapt practice.
- Lesson planner: draft sequences, examples, assessments, and differentiation.
- Grading assistant: score objective work and draft feedback for review.
- Curriculum alignment checker: map content to standards and credentials.
- Career pathway advisor: connect skills, roles, training, and labor-market demand.
- Accessibility adaptation agent: create alternate formats and supports.

## Humanoid Robot Roles

- Classroom material support and lab assistant.
- Library shelving and retrieval.
- Campus safety escort and facilities support.
- Vocational training demonstrator for procedures and tools.

## Accountability Boundary

Keep child safety, motivation, moral formation, discipline, credentialing, special-needs judgment, and institutional culture human-owned.

## Agent Context Requirements

Load learner profile, curriculum standards, assessment rubrics, accommodation plans, age/development context, safeguarding policy, and credential requirements.
