# Defense, Intelligence, Border, and Foreign Affairs Workforce

## Mission

Protect sovereignty, manage alliances, understand threats, control lawful movement, and negotiate with other polities.

## Core Jobs

- Detect, deter, defend, and recover from external threats.
- Negotiate alliances, treaties, and trade relationships.
- Verify identity, legality, safety, and compliance at borders.
- Gather and assess intelligence.
- Coordinate defense logistics, medicine, communications, and rules of engagement.

## Human Role Families

- Diplomat, foreign service officer, consular officer, trade representative.
- Intelligence analyst, OSINT analyst, linguist, threat analyst.
- Soldier, sailor, airman, marine, coast guard, defense planner.
- Border officer, customs specialist, immigration officer, export-control analyst.
- Defense engineer, logistics officer, acquisition manager, cyber operator.

## AI Personnel

- OSINT analyst agent: gather, translate, correlate, and summarize open-source signals.
- Sanctions and export-control screener: compare parties, goods, routes, and jurisdictions.
- Diplomatic brief agent: prepare country briefs, negotiation options, and treaty comparisons.
- Logistics optimizer: plan supply, transport, basing, and maintenance flows.
- Red-team simulation agent: model adversary options and vulnerabilities.
- Intelligence triage agent: prioritize reports and flag uncertainty.

## Humanoid Robot Roles

- Base logistics and warehouse support.
- Maintenance, inspection, and casualty evacuation support.
- Hazardous-area reconnaissance and disaster-relief unloading.
- Border facility support and inspection assistance.

## Accountability Boundary

Keep use of force, detention, asylum determinations, diplomacy, intelligence conclusions, escalation, and export-control enforcement human-commanded.

## Agent Context Requirements

Load current legal authorities, rules of engagement, classification boundaries, sanctions lists, export-control categories, treaties, diplomatic objectives, and escalation paths.
