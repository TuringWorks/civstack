# Household, Childcare, Eldercare, and Community Support Workforce

## Mission

Reproduce daily life: raise children, care for dependents, maintain homes, and prevent isolation.

## Core Jobs

- Feed, protect, teach, socialize, and love children.
- Preserve dignity, safety, autonomy, and connection for elders and disabled people.
- Handle cleaning, meals, repairs, scheduling, transportation, and care coordination.
- Connect people to housing, food, medical, legal, and social support.

## Human Role Families

- Parent, nanny, childcare worker, preschool teacher.
- Home health aide, personal care aide, eldercare coordinator.
- Social worker, case manager, community health worker.
- House cleaner, cook, handyman, family assistant.
- Nonprofit program manager, mutual aid coordinator, volunteer manager.

## AI Personnel

- Family scheduler: coordinate calendars, forms, reminders, and transport.
- Benefits navigator: match needs to programs and prepare applications.
- Care coordination agent: summarize visits, medications, appointments, and needs.
- Tutoring agent: support learning and practice.
- Medication reminder: prompt adherence and escalate missed doses.
- Social services referral agent: map needs to local resources.

## Humanoid Robot Roles

- Cleaning, laundry, meal prep assistance.
- Fetching, lifting support, mobility assistance, and monitoring.
- Routine companionship support, without replacing human relationship.

## Accountability Boundary

Keep parenting, intimate care consent, safeguarding, abuse detection, emotional bonding, medical decisions, and end-of-life care human-owned.

## Agent Context Requirements

Load household preferences, care plans, consent, emergency contacts, medication list, safeguarding rules, accessibility needs, benefit rules, and escalation instructions.
