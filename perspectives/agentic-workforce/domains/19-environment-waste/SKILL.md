# Environment, Climate, Waste, and Resource Stewardship Workforce

## Mission

Protect natural systems, manage waste, reduce pollution, and adapt to climate risk.

## Core Jobs

- Collect, sort, treat, recycle, compost, landfill, or neutralize waste.
- Monitor, enforce, remediate, and prevent pollution.
- Conserve, restore, and manage land, water, wildlife, and ecosystems.
- Forecast, adapt, insure, relocate, harden, and decarbonize under climate risk.

## Human Role Families

- Waste collection operator, recycling coordinator, landfill manager.
- Environmental scientist, conservation scientist, ecologist, hydrologist.
- Climate risk analyst, sustainability manager, carbon accounting specialist.
- Environmental compliance specialist, remediation project manager.
- Park ranger, natural resource manager, urban forester.

## AI Personnel

- Emissions accounting agent: calculate, reconcile, and report emissions.
- Satellite monitoring analyst: detect land-use, fire, water, and pollution changes.
- Climate risk modeler: estimate exposure, vulnerability, and adaptation options.
- Waste stream optimization agent: improve routing, sorting, and diversion.
- Permit compliance agent: compare evidence to environmental rules.
- Environmental impact review assistant: summarize impacts, alternatives, and mitigations.

## Humanoid Robot Roles

- Sorting facility support.
- Hazardous cleanup support.
- Field sampling and inspection.
- Park maintenance and restoration logistics.

## Accountability Boundary

Keep environmental justice, land-use tradeoffs, enforcement, relocation policy, protected-area governance, and remediation signoff human-led.

## Agent Context Requirements

Load permits, monitoring data, site maps, environmental standards, community impacts, climate scenarios, waste contracts, remediation plans, and public comment.
