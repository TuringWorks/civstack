# Public Finance, Tax, Treasury, and Procurement Workforce

## Mission

Collect revenue, allocate budgets, buy public goods, manage debt, distribute grants, and protect public money.

## Core Jobs

- Collect taxes and fees fairly.
- Prioritize budgets under scarcity.
- Procure goods and services transparently.
- Forecast cash flow, debt, pensions, and fiscal risk.
- Audit spending and report results.

## Human Role Families

- Tax examiner, revenue agent, tax policy analyst, collections specialist.
- Budget analyst, financial analyst, treasury analyst, grants manager.
- Procurement officer, contract specialist, vendor manager, sourcing analyst.
- Auditor, controller, forensic accountant, inspector general investigator.
- Economist, actuary, fiscal policy advisor, public pension analyst.

## AI Personnel

- Tax return review agent: detect anomalies and prepare review packets.
- Budget scenario agent: model fiscal choices and distributional effects.
- Procurement drafting agent: draft RFPs, scorecards, award memos, and contract clauses.
- Vendor risk analyst: screen sanctions, conflicts, financial health, and delivery history.
- Invoice reconciliation agent: match invoices, receipts, contracts, and approvals.
- Fraud detection agent: surface patterns for human investigation.

## Humanoid Robot Roles

- Mailroom and scanning support.
- Inventory and warehouse support for public assets.
- Physical asset inspection assistant.

## Accountability Boundary

Keep tax enforcement, budget authority, contract awards, debt issuance, audit opinions, fraud referrals, and public-fund allocation human-accountable.

## Agent Context Requirements

Load chart of accounts, procurement rules, delegation thresholds, audit standards, grant terms, vendor data, and conflict-of-interest policy before recommending action.
