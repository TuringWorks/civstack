# Governance, Law, and Public Administration Workforce

Use this skill to design accountable institutions that make rules, resolve disputes, deliver public services, preserve records, and maintain legitimacy.

## Mission

Create lawful rules, administer public programs, resolve disputes, preserve rights, and maintain citizen trust.

## Core Jobs

- Create rules when interests conflict.
- Determine eligibility and deliver public services.
- Resolve disputes with evidence and due process.
- Preserve records, transparency, and institutional memory.
- Update laws and standards as technology and risk change.

## Human Role Families

- Legislator, legislative aide, policy analyst, chief of staff.
- Public administrator, city manager, program manager, benefits specialist.
- Attorney, paralegal, legal operations manager, contract manager.
- Judge, magistrate, hearing officer, mediator, arbitrator.
- Court clerk, records manager, FOIA officer, administrative law specialist.
- Compliance officer, regulatory affairs manager, ethics officer, inspector general analyst.
- Civic technology product manager, government service designer, public-sector data analyst.

## AI Personnel

- Legislative research agent: compare statutes, summarize testimony, draft bill options.
- Benefits adjudication assistant: prepare case files, check documents, flag anomalies.
- Legal discovery agent: review evidence, contracts, timelines, and precedents.
- Public comment analyzer: cluster comments and extract stakeholder concerns.
- Records transparency agent: index, redact, classify, and prepare responses.
- Regulation drafting agent: convert policy intent into rule text and guidance.

## Humanoid Robot Roles

- Public-office concierge and queue assistant.
- Document runner and archive retrieval assistant.
- Facility inspection and security support under human supervision.

## Accountability Boundary

Keep lawmaking, judicial rulings, rights deprivation, benefit denial appeals, coercive enforcement, constitutional interpretation, and final regulatory penalties human-accountable.

## Agent Context Requirements

Load the relevant statute, policy, procedure, form, jurisdiction, appeal path, privacy rules, and public-record requirements before drafting or recommending action.
