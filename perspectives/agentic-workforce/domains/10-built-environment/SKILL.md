# Shelter, Construction, Land, and Built Environment Workforce

## Mission

Create and maintain places for living, working, mobility, commerce, and public life.

## Core Jobs

- Plan, finance, permit, build, inspect, and maintain shelter and workspaces.
- Balance housing, infrastructure, ecology, commerce, and fairness.
- Renovate, retrofit, or demolish aging buildings safely.
- Improve resilience to heat, fire, flood, wind, and seismic risk.

## Human Role Families

- Urban planner, zoning analyst, real estate developer, housing policy analyst.
- Architect, civil engineer, structural engineer, MEP engineer.
- Construction manager, superintendent, estimator, scheduler.
- Carpenter, electrician, plumber, HVAC technician, mason, roofer.
- Building inspector, code official, facilities manager, property manager.
- Surveyor, GIS analyst, land acquisition specialist.

## AI Personnel

- Permitting assistant: check completeness, route reviews, draft notices.
- Code compliance checker: compare drawings and specs to applicable codes.
- Construction scheduler: sequence trades, materials, inspections, and constraints.
- Design option generator: produce alternatives with cost, energy, and accessibility impacts.
- Quantity takeoff estimator: extract quantities from drawings/models.
- Facilities maintenance planner: prioritize repairs and lifecycle replacement.

## Humanoid Robot Roles

- Material handling, site cleanup, and inspection.
- Painting, drywall support, and repetitive tool tasks where safe.
- Facilities rounds, janitorial work, repair support, and disaster assessment.

## Accountability Boundary

Keep land-use decisions, structural signoff, occupancy approval, worker safety, eviction, public consultation, and code enforcement human-led.

## Agent Context Requirements

Load site conditions, zoning, codes, drawings, BIM, permits, inspection records, accessibility rules, labor safety plans, and public comments.
