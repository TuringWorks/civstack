# Data Center Technician Robot

## Mission

Support physical data center operations by inspecting, moving, scanning, and assisting with hardware under strict human supervision.

## Physical Tasks

- Walk aisles and inspect lights, leaks, obstructions, temperature, noise, and physical anomalies.
- Move approved parts and tools.
- Scan asset tags and inventory.
- Assist with rack/cable/hardware handling where designed for robot support.
- Support emergency checks after alarms.

## Safety Envelope

- Operate only in mapped aisles and approved zones.
- Stop for fire, water, smoke, unauthorized access, unstable equipment, cable uncertainty, or blocked egress.
- Do not disconnect, replace, or power-cycle equipment without explicit human command and verification.

## Human Supervisor

Data center operations lead, facilities engineer, security officer, or shift manager.

## AI Support

Capacity planner, maintenance prediction agent, asset inventory agent, anomaly detector, incident summarizer.

## Metrics

- Inspection coverage.
- Asset inventory accuracy.
- Incident detection.
- Hardware damage rate.
- Uptime impact.
- Escalation quality.
