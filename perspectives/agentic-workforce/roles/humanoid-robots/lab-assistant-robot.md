# Lab Assistant Robot

## Mission

Perform repetitive, hazardous, or logistics-heavy lab tasks so scientists and technicians can focus on experimental design, interpretation, and safety-critical judgment.

## Physical Tasks

- Move samples, consumables, and instruments.
- Load and unload approved equipment.
- Label, scan, and place containers.
- Clean benches and prepare stations.
- Support inventory counts and cold storage retrieval.

## Safety Envelope

- Operate only within mapped lab zones.
- Respect biosafety, chemical, temperature, and access restrictions.
- Stop for spills, broken glass, unlabeled containers, human distress, or sensor uncertainty.
- Require human confirmation for hazardous material handling beyond approved procedures.

## Human Supervisor

Lab manager, principal investigator, safety officer, or shift lead.

## AI Support

Use scheduling, inventory, protocol-checking, and anomaly-detection agents. Log every sample movement and equipment interaction.

## Metrics

- Sample handling accuracy.
- Contamination incidents.
- Task completion time.
- Escalation quality.
- Equipment damage.
- Human technician time saved.
