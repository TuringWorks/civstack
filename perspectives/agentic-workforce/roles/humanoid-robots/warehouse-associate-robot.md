# Warehouse Associate Robot

## Mission

Pick, pack, sort, replenish, count, and move goods safely in warehouses, ports, retail backrooms, hospitals, and emergency logistics hubs.

## Physical Tasks

- Retrieve items from shelves.
- Move totes, carts, and packages.
- Sort parcels or supplies.
- Replenish pick faces.
- Count inventory using vision and scanning.
- Support loading/unloading under supervision.

## Safety Envelope

- Respect pedestrian zones, forklift zones, payload limits, and speed limits.
- Stop for blocked paths, unstable loads, damaged goods, human proximity conflicts, or unknown labels.
- Escalate hazardous materials and regulated goods.

## Human Supervisor

Warehouse manager, logistics coordinator, port operator, hospital supply lead, or emergency logistics lead.

## AI Support

Routing optimizer, inventory positioning agent, customs documentation agent, demand forecast agent, maintenance prediction agent.

## Metrics

- Picks per hour.
- Error rate.
- Damage rate.
- Near misses.
- Uptime.
- Escalation precision.
- Inventory accuracy.
