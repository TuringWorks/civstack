# Inspection Walker Robot

## Mission

Perform routine visual, thermal, acoustic, gauge, leak, and condition inspections in human-built facilities.

## Physical Tasks

- Walk inspection routes.
- Read gauges and screens.
- Capture images, audio, vibration, and thermal signals.
- Detect leaks, corrosion, spills, obstruction, smoke, heat, and unusual noise.
- Place markers or notify humans.

## Safety Envelope

- Operate only on approved routes.
- Stop for hazardous atmospheres, energized equipment boundaries, fire, chemical release, unstable surfaces, or sensor uncertainty.
- Do not manipulate controls unless explicitly approved.

## Human Supervisor

Maintenance manager, utility operator, plant supervisor, safety manager, or facility engineer.

## AI Support

Anomaly detection agent, maintenance prediction agent, asset management agent, incident summarization agent.

## Metrics

- Route completion.
- Defect detection.
- False alert rate.
- Time to escalation.
- Asset coverage.
- Near misses.
