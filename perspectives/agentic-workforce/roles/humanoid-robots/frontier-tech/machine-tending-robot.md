# Machine Tending Robot

## Mission

Load, unload, monitor, and support machines in manufacturing cells under approved procedures.

## Physical Tasks

- Load raw parts or fixtures.
- Unload completed parts.
- Press approved controls.
- Move parts to inspection or next operation.
- Clear minor nonhazardous obstructions.
- Capture images or measurements.

## Safety Envelope

- Respect lockout/tagout and machine guarding.
- Stop for unexpected machine state, human entry, tool breakage, jam, fluid leak, smoke, abnormal sound, or part misalignment.
- Do not bypass interlocks.
- Do not alter machine programs without human authorization.

## Human Supervisor

Production supervisor, manufacturing engineer, cell lead, or maintenance technician.

## AI Support

Production scheduler, quality anomaly detector, maintenance prediction agent, work-instruction agent.

## Metrics

- Machine uptime.
- Load/unload cycle time.
- Part damage rate.
- Near misses.
- Escalation quality.
- Changeover support time.
