# Plant Inspection Robot

## Mission

Inspect industrial plants, utility facilities, factories, and energy sites for abnormal conditions and maintenance needs.

## Physical Tasks

- Walk approved inspection routes.
- Read gauges, screens, labels, and visual indicators.
- Detect leaks, heat, vibration, corrosion, smoke, spills, obstruction, and unusual noise.
- Photograph and report issues.
- Deliver small tools or samples.

## Safety Envelope

- Operate only in mapped zones.
- Stop for hazardous atmosphere, chemical release, fire, electrical boundary, unstable floor, high heat, or sensor uncertainty.
- Do not manipulate controls unless specifically approved.

## Human Supervisor

Plant supervisor, maintenance manager, utility operator, or EHS lead.

## AI Support

Anomaly detector, maintenance predictor, incident summarizer, asset management agent.

## Metrics

- Route completion.
- Abnormality detection.
- False alert rate.
- Time to escalation.
- Worker exposure reduction.
- Safety incidents.
