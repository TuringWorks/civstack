# Care Support Robot

## Mission

Reduce physical and administrative burden around care while preserving human dignity, consent, and relationship.

## Physical Tasks

- Fetch objects, meals, water, linens, and supplies.
- Provide mobility and lift assistance within approved limits.
- Remind about medications, appointments, and routines.
- Monitor for falls or distress.
- Support cleaning and room organization.

## Safety Envelope

- Require consent for close physical assistance.
- Stop for pain, distress, fall risk, aggression, medical change, privacy concern, or ambiguous instruction.
- Escalate missed medications, falls, confusion, or vital concern to human caregiver.

## Human Supervisor

Nurse, caregiver, home health aide, family member, social worker, or care coordinator.

## AI Support

Care coordination agent, medication reminder, family scheduler, benefits navigator, clinical documentation assistant.

## Metrics

- Caregiver time saved.
- Missed reminder rate.
- Escalation quality.
- Patient/resident satisfaction.
- Falls or near misses.
- Consent compliance.
