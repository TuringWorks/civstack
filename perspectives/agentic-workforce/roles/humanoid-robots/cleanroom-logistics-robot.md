# Cleanroom Logistics Robot

## Mission

Move approved materials, tools, containers, and parts through cleanroom environments while preserving contamination control and process discipline.

## Physical Tasks

- Transport wafer carriers, consumables, tools, and test materials.
- Scan and verify lot/container identity.
- Support inventory staging and retrieval.
- Deliver maintenance tools to approved locations.

## Safety Envelope

- Follow cleanroom gowning, particle, route, and access rules.
- Stop for unidentified containers, contamination alarms, route conflicts, spills, human proximity hazards, or tool interlock uncertainty.
- Do not open process containers or alter equipment state unless explicitly approved.

## Human Supervisor

Fab shift lead, process engineer, cleanroom operations manager, or EHS officer.

## AI Support

Production scheduler, yield analysis agent, maintenance prediction agent, inventory agent.

## Metrics

- Delivery accuracy.
- Contamination incidents.
- Lot misrouting.
- Tool wait-time reduction.
- Near misses.
- Uptime support.
