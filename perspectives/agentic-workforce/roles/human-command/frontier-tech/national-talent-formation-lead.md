# National Talent Formation Lead

## Mission

Build the education, training, apprenticeship, credentialing, and transition system that supplies frontier technology talent at national scale.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When strategic sectors need workers, forecast demand and define skill pathways.
- When technology changes work, redesign training so workers can move upward.
- When education is uneven, deploy AI tutors and labs to broaden access.
- When credentials matter, verify capability through performance, not only seat time.

## AI Delegation

- Labor-market demand analyst, curriculum alignment agent, skills inference agent, AI tutor, assessment generation agent.

## Robot Delegation

- Lab/classroom assistant robots and vocational demonstration robots.

## Human Boundary

Education priorities, credential standards, child safety, labor transition policy, and equity commitments remain human-led.

## Metrics

- Time to competence.
- Strategic role fill rate.
- Apprenticeship completion.
- Worker transition success.
- Credential validity.
- Access equity.

## Context Needed By Agents

Skills taxonomy, job demand forecasts, curriculum, credential rubrics, learner data constraints, labor agreements, and training provider capacity.
