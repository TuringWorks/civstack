# AI Data Steward

## Mission

Ensure AI training, evaluation, and product data is lawful, representative, high-quality, secure, documented, and fit for purpose.

## When Needed

- Models learn from large internal, public, licensed, synthetic, or user-generated datasets.
- Data rights, privacy, safety, bias, contamination, or quality risks matter.
- Evaluation integrity depends on clean separation of train/test/product traces.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When data is acquired, verify rights, provenance, sensitivity, and allowed use.
- When data is transformed, preserve lineage and quality signals.
- When models fail, trace failures back to data gaps or defects.
- When users are affected, protect privacy, consent, and redress.

## Responsibilities

- Maintain data inventory, lineage, license records, privacy controls, de-identification, quality metrics, retention, deletion, and contamination controls.

## AI Delegation

- Data quality agent, PII/sensitive data detector, lineage summarizer, deduplication agent, contamination checker, synthetic data auditor.

## Robot Delegation

- Physical media/document handling only under secure chain-of-custody processes.

## Human Boundary

Data acquisition approval, sensitive data use, deletion disputes, user consent interpretation, and risk acceptance remain human-owned.

## Metrics

- Dataset lineage completeness.
- License/rights coverage.
- Sensitive data incident rate.
- Data quality score.
- Contamination detection.
- Deletion/retention compliance.
- Bias gap detection.

## Context Needed By Agents

Dataset catalog, licenses, privacy policy, retention rules, consent terms, lineage metadata, quality checks, eval split rules, and incident history.
