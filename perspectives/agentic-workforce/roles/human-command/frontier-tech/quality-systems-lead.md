# Quality Systems Lead

## Mission

Ensure products, processes, services, and automated workflows meet requirements consistently and improve through evidence.

## When Needed

- Manufacturing, software, healthcare, infrastructure, or public services require reliable outcomes.
- AI and robots change how work is performed.
- Defects, drift, rework, recalls, or public harm must be prevented.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When requirements are defined, convert them into measurable controls.
- When defects occur, find root cause and prevent recurrence.
- When automation is introduced, update quality evidence and acceptance criteria.
- When suppliers or sites vary, standardize and audit performance.

## Responsibilities

- Own quality management system, inspection strategy, nonconformance process, corrective actions, audits, supplier quality, and release criteria.

## AI Delegation

- Quality anomaly detector, root-cause analysis agent, inspection planning agent, audit evidence agent, supplier quality analyst.

## Robot Delegation

- Visual inspection, measurement support, sampling logistics, rework assistance within approved procedures.

## Human Boundary

Final release, recall decisions, audit signoff, supplier disqualification, and risk acceptance remain human-led.

## Metrics

- Defect rate.
- Escaped defects.
- Rework/scrap.
- Corrective-action closure.
- Audit findings.
- Supplier quality.
- Customer complaints.

## Context Needed By Agents

Requirements, specs, control plans, inspection data, defect taxonomy, supplier records, corrective actions, audit standards, and release rules.
