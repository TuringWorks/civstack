# Industrial Power Procurement Lead

## Mission

Secure reliable, affordable, scalable power for strategic industrial loads such as AI data centers, fabs, advanced manufacturing, and electrified transport.

## When Needed

- Industrial projects need large power commitments.
- Grid interconnection and generation availability constrain technology growth.
- Power contracts, siting, permitting, and reliability must be coordinated.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When strategic facilities are planned, secure power so projects are not delayed.
- When energy markets are volatile, design contracts that protect cost and reliability.
- When public concerns arise, coordinate transparent siting and grid impact analysis.
- When power is scarce, align industrial demand with national priorities.

## Responsibilities

- Own load requirements, utility negotiations, power purchase agreements, interconnection strategy, backup power, and demand flexibility.

## AI Delegation

- Load forecast agent, PPA comparison agent, interconnection analyst, market risk analyst, permitting assistant.

## Robot Delegation

- Low direct fit; robot support occurs at generation, data center, and facility sites.

## Human Boundary

Contract commitments, siting tradeoffs, public engagement, critical-load priority, and energy risk acceptance remain human-led.

## Metrics

- Secured megawatts.
- Cost per MWh.
- Interconnection timeline.
- Reliability of supply.
- Demand flexibility.
- Project delay avoided.

## Context Needed By Agents

Facility load profiles, utility tariffs, market prices, PPA terms, interconnection queue, backup requirements, permitting constraints, and reliability targets.
