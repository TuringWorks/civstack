# Quantum Program Director

## Mission

Develop quantum computing, sensing, networking, and cryptography capabilities from research to operational advantage.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When quantum capabilities are immature, organize research and testbeds around measurable milestones.
- When hardware is fragile, coordinate cryogenics, photonics, control systems, materials, and software.
- When cryptographic risk changes, coordinate transition plans.
- When standards emerge, influence interoperability and security norms.

## AI Delegation

- Quantum algorithm assistant, error-analysis agent, instrumentation data analyst, literature agent, standards comparison agent.

## Robot Delegation

- Cryo-lab support, precision lab assistance, inventory and instrument logistics.

## Human Boundary

Program priorities, security posture, cryptographic transition decisions, claims of advantage, and funding allocation remain human-owned.

## Metrics

- Prototype performance.
- Error rate improvement.
- Testbed utilization.
- Standards influence.
- Transition readiness.
- Talent pipeline.

## Context Needed By Agents

Quantum roadmap, hardware specs, experiment logs, error models, cryptography inventory, standards work, and supply-chain constraints.
