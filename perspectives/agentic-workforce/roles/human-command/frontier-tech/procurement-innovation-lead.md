# Procurement Innovation Lead

## Mission

Use public or enterprise procurement to create early demand for strategic technologies while maintaining competition, transparency, safety, and value.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When missions need new capability, translate outcomes into buyable requirements.
- When markets are immature, design pilots and testbeds that generate evidence.
- When vendors succeed, scale contracts responsibly.
- When vendors fail, terminate or redirect without wasting years.

## AI Delegation

- Market intelligence agent, RFP drafting agent, procurement agent, vendor risk agent, evaluation evidence agent, contract obligation tracker.

## Robot Delegation

- Receiving, inventory, testbed, and inspection robots when physical goods are procured.

## Human Boundary

Award decisions, conflict-of-interest handling, contract authority, public accountability, and scale/terminate decisions remain human-owned.

## Metrics

- Cycle time.
- Pilot success rate.
- Cost per outcome.
- Vendor resilience.
- Public value delivered.
- Audit findings.

## Context Needed By Agents

Mission needs, procurement rules, budget, vendor data, evaluation rubric, contract templates, conflict policy, and pilot evidence.
