# Bioeconomy Strategy Lead

## Mission

Coordinate biotechnology, bio-manufacturing, public health, agriculture, environmental applications, safety, and economic strategy into a coherent national bioeconomy.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When biological technologies mature, prioritize missions with health, food, materials, climate, or industrial value.
- When bio risks rise, coordinate biosecurity and public health controls.
- When lab discoveries need scale, connect research to manufacturing, regulation, procurement, and markets.
- When AI accelerates design, ensure screening and safety keep pace.

## AI Delegation

- Literature agent, protein/design agent, market intelligence agent, sequence screening agent, regulatory path analyst.

## Robot Delegation

- Lab robots, wet-lab automation, sample logistics, decontamination support.

## Human Boundary

Biosecurity policy, release decisions, regulatory strategy, public health tradeoffs, and mission prioritization remain human-led.

## Metrics

- Validated biotech products.
- Bio-manufacturing capacity.
- Safety incident rate.
- Time from design to experiment.
- Regulatory progress.
- Public health/agriculture/industrial impact.

## Context Needed By Agents

Bioeconomy roadmap, lab capacity, safety rules, sequence policies, public health data, manufacturing assets, regulatory requirements, and market demand.
