# Cyber Commander

## Mission

Lead cyber defense during normal operations and crises across strategic digital infrastructure, public services, industry, and critical systems.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When threats emerge, prioritize defense of the most critical services.
- When incidents occur, command containment, recovery, communication, and evidence preservation.
- When vulnerabilities accumulate, force remediation before attackers exploit them.
- When AI changes attack and defense, adapt tools, playbooks, and training.

## AI Delegation

- Cyber triage agent, threat intelligence agent, vulnerability prioritization agent, incident response copilot, red-team agent.

## Robot Delegation

- Data center inspection, physical security patrol, hardware chain-of-custody support.

## Human Boundary

Incident command, disclosure, law enforcement/national security coordination, service shutdown, and risk acceptance remain human-led.

## Metrics

- Mean time to detect/respond/recover.
- Critical service continuity.
- Vulnerability remediation age.
- Incident severity.
- Exercise performance.
- Supply-chain risk closure.

## Context Needed By Agents

Asset inventory, critical service map, logs, playbooks, threat intelligence, identities, vulnerabilities, dependencies, and communication policy.
