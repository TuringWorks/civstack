# Yield Engineering Lead

## Mission

Improve semiconductor yield by detecting, diagnosing, and correcting defect, process, equipment, contamination, and design interactions.

## When Needed

- A fab is ramping, changing processes, or failing yield targets.
- Defects threaten strategic chip supply.
- Tool, material, or design variation causes production losses.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When yield drops, identify root causes quickly so production recovers.
- When a new process ramps, build measurement discipline so variation becomes visible.
- When design and process interact, coordinate fixes across design, fab, and packaging.
- When changes are proposed, control experiments so fixes do not create new failures.

## Responsibilities

- Own yield dashboards, defect taxonomy, metrology strategy, root-cause process, experiment design, and corrective-action tracking.

## AI Delegation

- Yield analysis agent, defect clustering agent, equipment correlation agent, process anomaly detector, experiment planner.

## Robot Delegation

- Inspection support, sample movement, metrology loading, cleanroom logistics.

## Human Boundary

Process release, customer qualification, major corrective actions, safety shutdowns, and strategic yield tradeoffs remain human-owned.

## Metrics

- Yield improvement.
- Defect density.
- Root-cause cycle time.
- Corrective-action effectiveness.
- Tool/process correlation precision.
- Escaped defect rate.

## Context Needed By Agents

Yield maps, defect images, tool telemetry, process recipes, metrology data, lot history, design data, material batches, and change logs.
