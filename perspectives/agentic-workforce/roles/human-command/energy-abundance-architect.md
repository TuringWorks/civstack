# Energy Abundance Architect

## Mission

Create the energy capacity, reliability, affordability, and resilience needed for frontier industry, compute, electrification, and modern life.

## When Needed

- AI compute, manufacturing, transport, heating, and industrial loads grow.
- Grid reliability, permitting, fuel supply, or generation capacity constrains technology leadership.
- Decarbonization and industrial growth must be reconciled.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When technology demand grows, plan power supply so energy does not bottleneck national ambition.
- When grids become complex, integrate generation, storage, transmission, demand response, and flexible loads.
- When outages or fuel shocks occur, preserve essential services and industrial continuity.
- When projects stall, coordinate permitting, finance, public trust, and interconnection.

## Responsibilities

- Develop energy roadmaps, siting strategy, utility coordination, storage/transmission plans, resilience requirements, and industrial power deals.

## AI Delegation

- Load forecast agent, interconnection analyst, grid simulation agent, permitting assistant, outage planner, market analyst.

## Robot Delegation

- Plant/site inspection, solar/wind maintenance support, warehouse logistics, substation visual inspection under strict rules.

## Human Boundary

Grid emergency authority, nuclear safety, siting tradeoffs, public consultation, rate design, and critical-load prioritization remain human-owned.

## Metrics

- Cost of energy.
- Reliability indices.
- Interconnection time.
- New capacity delivered.
- Industrial load served.
- Emissions intensity.
- Recovery time after outage.

## Context Needed By Agents

Load forecasts, grid topology, generation assets, project pipeline, permits, land constraints, market rules, reliability standards, and public engagement records.
