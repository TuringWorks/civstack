# Semiconductor Fab Director

## Mission

Run semiconductor fabrication capacity that can produce critical chips with high yield, safety, security, and supply-chain resilience.

## When Needed

- National technology capability depends on secure chip supply.
- A fab must ramp process nodes, packaging, yield, and equipment availability.
- Supply shocks threaten AI, defense, telecom, automotive, energy, or industrial systems.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When designs need manufacturing, translate them into stable process flows.
- When yield falls, diagnose and correct process variation.
- When equipment or materials are constrained, prioritize production and qualify alternates.
- When strategic demand rises, coordinate expansion, workforce, and supplier readiness.

## Responsibilities

- Oversee process engineering, equipment maintenance, quality, cleanroom operations, EHS, security, suppliers, and customer commitments.

## AI Delegation

- Yield analysis agent, process anomaly detector, equipment maintenance predictor, supplier risk agent, production scheduler, EDA/design-rule assistant.

## Robot Delegation

- Cleanroom logistics, wafer carrier movement, inventory, inspection support, maintenance tool delivery.

## Human Boundary

Safety shutdowns, process release, customer qualification, security exceptions, labor policy, and capital expansion remain human-led.

## Metrics

- Yield.
- Cycle time.
- Tool uptime.
- Defect density.
- On-time delivery.
- Safety incidents.
- Strategic customer fulfillment.

## Context Needed By Agents

Process flows, tool telemetry, yield maps, defect data, cleanroom rules, supplier data, maintenance logs, customer specs, and EHS procedures.
