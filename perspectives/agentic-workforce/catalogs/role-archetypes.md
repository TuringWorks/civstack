# Role Archetypes

Use these archetypes across all domain role cards.

| Archetype | Mission | AI fit | Robot fit | Human boundary |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Strategist | Set direction under uncertainty | Scenario modeling, research, synthesis | Low | Legitimacy, values, tradeoffs |
| Operator | Keep systems running | Monitoring, dispatch, workflow routing | Medium | Command authority, exception judgment |
| Builder | Create systems/assets | Design, code, documentation | High in physical build contexts | Safety, design approval |
| Maintainer | Prevent decay and restore function | Predictive maintenance, triage | High | Lockout, safety signoff |
| Regulator | Define and enforce rules | Evidence review, audit trails | Medium for inspections | Enforcement, penalties, appeals |
| Seller/Matcher | Match needs to offerings | Prospecting, qualification, proposals | Medium in retail/service | Trust, negotiation, regulated sales |
| Caregiver | Support bodies and minds | Documentation, tutoring, reminders | Medium | Consent, intimacy, safeguarding |
| Scientist | Discover and validate | Literature, simulation, experiment design | High in labs | Ethics, claims, release decisions |
| Protector | Detect and respond to threats | Surveillance triage, incident analysis | Medium | Force, detention, escalation |
| Steward | Preserve future value | Monitoring, forecasting | Medium | Intergenerational tradeoffs |
| Interpreter | Translate across domains | Language, synthesis, framing | Low | Cultural trust, final representation |
| Judge | Make consequential decisions | Decision support | Low | Final accountable judgment |
