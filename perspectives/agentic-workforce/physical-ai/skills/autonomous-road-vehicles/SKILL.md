# Autonomous Road Vehicles Workforce

## Mission

Move people and goods safely, efficiently, and reliably on roads, campuses, depots, ports, industrial sites, and logistics corridors.

## Best Use Cases

- Hub-to-hub autonomous freight.
- Port drayage and yard trucks.
- Campus, airport, hospital, and business-park shuttles.
- Fixed-route public transit in mapped areas.
- Last-mile delivery vehicles in controlled neighborhoods or campuses.
- Autonomous fleet repositioning and depot operations.

## Human Role Families

- Fleet safety operator, remote assistance operator, autonomous vehicle operations manager.
- Safety case engineer, ODD manager, mapping lead, simulation lead.
- Vehicle maintenance lead, field operations technician, incident investigator.
- Transit operations lead, freight operations lead, depot manager.
- Regulatory affairs lead, insurance/risk manager, public engagement lead.

## AI Personnel

- Route risk analyst: evaluate route complexity, weather, traffic, construction, and crash history.
- Fleet dispatch agent: assign vehicles, missions, charging/fueling, and maintenance windows.
- Perception failure analyst: review disengagements, near misses, and edge cases.
- Simulation scenario generator: convert real-world events into regression scenarios.
- Incident response copilot: summarize event logs, video, telemetry, and operator actions.
- Maintenance prediction agent: forecast service needs from vehicle telemetry.

## Autonomous Machine Roles

- Autonomous truck: hub-to-hub freight, yard movement, port drayage.
- Autonomous shuttle/bus: fixed-route passenger movement.
- Robotaxi: passenger trips within approved operating design domain.
- Autonomous delivery vehicle: parcel, grocery, medical, campus delivery.
- Autonomous yard tractor: trailer movement in depots and warehouses.

## Operating Design Domain

Define geography, road types, speed range, weather, lighting, traffic density, construction handling, emergency vehicle behavior, passenger policy, cargo policy, and remote assistance requirements.

## Safety And Accountability

Keep public-road deployment approval, route approval, passenger safety, incident disclosure, regulatory engagement, and emergency response human-accountable.

## Required Context For Agents

Maps, ODD definition, vehicle capability, traffic rules, route plans, dispatch data, weather, telemetry, disengagement logs, maintenance records, insurance requirements, and incident playbooks.
