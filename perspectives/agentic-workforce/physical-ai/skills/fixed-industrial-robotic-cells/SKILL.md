# Fixed Industrial Robotic Cells Workforce

## Mission

Automate repeatable physical work where the environment can be engineered around the machine.

## Best Use Cases

- Assembly, welding, painting, dispensing, polishing, and packaging.
- Machine tending and part transfer.
- Pick-and-place and sorting.
- Machine vision inspection.
- Lab automation and sample processing.
- Food processing and packaging.
- Hazardous or precision repetitive tasks.

## Human Role Families

- Automation engineering lead, robotic cell engineer, controls engineer.
- Process engineer, quality systems lead, maintenance technician.
- Safety engineer, production supervisor, workforce training lead.

## AI Personnel

- Cell design assistant: propose layout, tooling, cycle time, and safety envelope.
- Vision inspection agent: detect defects and process drift.
- Robot program assistant: generate or review robot paths and routines.
- Maintenance prediction agent: monitor actuators, grippers, sensors, and cycle anomalies.
- Quality anomaly detector: connect cell data to defects and corrective actions.

## Autonomous Machine Roles

- Welding robot cell.
- Painting/coating robot cell.
- Pick-and-place robot cell.
- Vision inspection cell.
- Lab automation cell.
- Food processing cell.
- Collaborative robot workstation.

## Operating Design Domain

Define fixtures, part variation, tooling, guarding, human interaction, speed/force limits, lockout, quality criteria, cycle time, and maintenance access.

## Safety And Accountability

Keep safety certification, lockout/tagout, final quality release, worker training, and process change approval human-accountable.

## Required Context For Agents

Process plan, CAD, fixtures, robot programs, PLC logic, safety standards, quality specs, maintenance logs, incident records, and worker training materials.
