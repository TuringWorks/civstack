# Drones And Aerial Robotics Workforce

## Mission

Use aerial autonomy to sense, inspect, map, deliver, spray, monitor, and respond where airborne perspective or mobility creates value.

## Best Use Cases

- Crop scouting, spraying, seeding, and livestock monitoring.
- Utility, solar, wind, roof, bridge, pipeline, and rail inspection.
- Disaster mapping, search support, fire monitoring, and damage assessment.
- Medical, parcel, and emergency supply delivery in defined corridors.
- Construction progress mapping and site safety monitoring.
- Environmental monitoring and conservation.

## Human Role Families

- Drone operations lead, remote pilot/operator, airspace compliance lead.
- Fleet maintenance technician, payload specialist, mission planner.
- Inspection program lead, emergency response coordinator, privacy/safety officer.

## AI Personnel

- Flight mission planner: plan route, altitude, payload, battery, weather, and airspace.
- Image analysis agent: detect defects, crop stress, damage, fire, people, animals, or hazards.
- Airspace risk agent: monitor airspace, no-fly zones, terrain, and weather.
- Fleet scheduling agent: assign drones, batteries, pilots, payloads, and maintenance.
- Incident analysis agent: review flight logs, video, telemetry, and communications.

## Autonomous Machine Roles

- Agricultural scouting drone.
- Spray/seeding drone.
- Inspection drone.
- Delivery drone.
- Public safety/disaster response drone.
- Mapping/survey drone.

## Operating Design Domain

Define airspace, altitude, route, geofence, weather, visibility, payload, communications, battery reserve, emergency landing zones, people/animal overflight rules, and data privacy limits.

## Safety And Accountability

Keep airspace authorization, public safety, privacy, chemical application, emergency response use, and incident disclosure human-accountable.

## Required Context For Agents

Mission plan, maps, airspace constraints, weather, payload specs, drone capability, battery status, flight logs, image data, privacy policy, and emergency procedures.
