# Autonomous Loaders And Heavy Equipment Workforce

## Mission

Move earth, ore, aggregate, containers, debris, and bulk materials safely and productively in mines, quarries, construction sites, ports, farms, and disaster zones.

## Best Use Cases

- Autonomous mine haul trucks.
- Autonomous wheel loaders and mile/mine loaders.
- Autonomous excavators, dozers, graders, and compactors.
- Quarry and aggregate movement.
- Port container and bulk-material movement.
- Disaster debris removal and hazardous site work.
- Repetitive construction earthmoving and grading.

## Human Role Families

- Heavy equipment autonomy lead, mine autonomy manager, site autonomy superintendent.
- Equipment dispatcher, remote operator, safety supervisor, maintenance lead.
- Survey/GIS lead, geotechnical engineer, construction superintendent.
- Incident investigator, environmental compliance lead.

## AI Personnel

- Site mission planner: convert design/survey plan into machine tasks and routes.
- Terrain and stability analyst: detect slope, soil, load, and hazard risks.
- Fleet dispatch agent: coordinate machines, trucks, crushers, stockpiles, and maintenance.
- Payload optimization agent: optimize bucket loads, haul cycles, and fuel/energy.
- Maintenance prediction agent: monitor hydraulics, tires/tracks, engines, sensors, and implements.
- Safety zone monitor: detect people, vehicles, animals, and exclusion violations.

## Autonomous Machine Roles

- Autonomous loader.
- Autonomous mine/mile loader.
- Autonomous haul truck.
- Autonomous excavator.
- Autonomous dozer.
- Autonomous grader.
- Autonomous compactor.
- Autonomous port/bulk-material handler.

## Operating Design Domain

Define site map, haul roads, stockpiles, benches, slopes, geotechnical hazards, visibility, weather, blast zones, pedestrian/vehicle exclusions, payload limits, and emergency response.

## Safety And Accountability

Keep site safety, blast/ground control, public-road interaction, geotechnical risk, environmental compliance, worker exclusion zones, and incident command human-accountable.

## Required Context For Agents

Site map, survey model, task plan, machine capability, payload specs, geotechnical data, traffic plan, exclusion zones, weather, maintenance logs, and safety procedures.
