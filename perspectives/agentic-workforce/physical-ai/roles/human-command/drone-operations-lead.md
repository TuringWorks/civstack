# Drone Operations Lead

## Mission

Operate drone fleets safely and usefully across inspection, agriculture, delivery, public safety, mapping, and environmental missions.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When aerial data or delivery is needed, select mission type, payload, route, and airspace plan.
- When conditions change, delay, reroute, or cancel flights.
- When imagery or sensor data arrives, convert it into decisions.
- When privacy or safety concerns arise, enforce boundaries and escalate.

## AI Delegation

- Flight mission planner, airspace risk agent, image analysis agent, weather risk agent, fleet scheduling agent, privacy redaction agent.

## Machine Delegation

- Inspection drones, agricultural drones, delivery drones, disaster response drones, mapping drones, security drones.

## Human Boundary

Airspace authorization, flight approval, emergency response, chemical payload use, privacy policy, and incident disclosure remain human-led.

## Metrics

- Mission completion.
- Safety incidents.
- Airspace compliance.
- Data quality.
- Battery margin.
- Detection accuracy.
- Privacy incidents.

## Context Needed By Agents

Flight plan, airspace rules, weather, payload, drone capability, maps, privacy policy, imagery, flight logs, and emergency procedures.
