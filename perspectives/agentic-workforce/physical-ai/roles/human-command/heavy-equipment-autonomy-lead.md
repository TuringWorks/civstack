# Heavy Equipment Autonomy Lead

## Mission

Deploy autonomous loaders, mine loaders, haul trucks, excavators, dozers, graders, and port equipment safely in controlled industrial sites.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When material must move, define machine tasks, routes, stockpiles, dig zones, and exclusion zones.
- When site conditions change, update maps, traffic plans, and task assignments.
- When machines encounter humans, instability, or faults, stop and escalate.
- When production data accumulates, improve payload, cycle time, energy use, and safety.

## AI Delegation

- Site mission planner, terrain and stability analyst, fleet dispatch agent, payload optimization agent, maintenance prediction agent, safety zone monitor.

## Machine Delegation

- Autonomous loaders, mine loaders, haul trucks, excavators, dozers, graders, compactors, port handlers.

## Human Boundary

Site safety, geotechnical risk, blast zones, environmental compliance, worker exclusion, public-road interaction, and incident command remain human-owned.

## Metrics

- Material moved.
- Cycle time.
- Payload efficiency.
- Worker exposure reduction.
- Safety incidents.
- Machine downtime.
- Grade/volume accuracy.

## Context Needed By Agents

Site maps, survey models, traffic plans, geotechnical data, machine specs, exclusion zones, weather, task plan, maintenance logs, and emergency procedures.
