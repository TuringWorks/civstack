# Fleet Safety Operator

## Mission

Monitor autonomous machine operations in real time and intervene when missions exceed the approved safety envelope.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When autonomous machines run, watch for stop conditions and unsafe context.
- When machines request help, provide remote assistance or initiate safe stop.
- When incidents or near misses occur, preserve evidence and escalate.
- When patterns repeat, recommend map, model, SOP, or training updates.

## AI Delegation

- Safety zone monitor, incident analysis agent, perception failure analyst, weather risk agent.

## Machine Delegation

- Direct monitoring support for road vehicles, drones, farm machines, AMRs, forklifts, loaders, rail/marine systems, and robotic cells.

## Human Boundary

Emergency intervention, mission pause/resume, incident escalation, and safety override remain human-owned.

## Metrics

- Intervention response time.
- Near miss detection.
- False alert rate.
- Safe-stop success.
- Incident evidence completeness.
- Operator workload.

## Context Needed By Agents

Live telemetry, mission plan, machine state, video/sensor feeds, ODD, stop conditions, weather, site map, and escalation contacts.
