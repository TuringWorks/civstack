# Freight Autonomy Lead

## Mission

Coordinate autonomous trucks, yard tractors, delivery vehicles, warehouse robots, ports, and compliance workflows into a reliable freight network.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When freight demand arrives, choose where autonomy can safely improve cost, speed, or reliability.
- When cargo crosses modes or borders, coordinate handoffs and documentation.
- When autonomous routes are constrained, maintain fallback carriers and human operations.
- When incidents occur, protect people, cargo, customers, and compliance evidence.

## AI Delegation

- Routing optimizer, fleet dispatch agent, customs documentation agent, route/geofence risk analyst, maintenance prediction agent.

## Machine Delegation

- Autonomous trucks, yard tractors, delivery vehicles, AMRs, forklifts, port handlers, delivery drones.

## Human Boundary

Route approval, customer commitments, hazardous/regulated cargo, import/export compliance, incident disclosure, and public-road deployment remain human-led.

## Metrics

On-time delivery, freight cost, safety incidents, cargo damage, clearance time, fleet utilization, route availability.

## Context Needed By Agents

Orders, cargo type, routes, maps, customs documents, vehicle capability, warehouse/port data, customer SLAs, and incident playbooks.
