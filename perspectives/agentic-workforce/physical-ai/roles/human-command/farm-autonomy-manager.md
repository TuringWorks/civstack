# Farm Autonomy Manager

## Mission

Operate autonomous farm machines and agricultural drones so crop production improves without compromising worker safety, crop quality, soil health, animal welfare, or environmental stewardship.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When field work is needed, convert agronomic intent into safe autonomous missions.
- When weather and crop stage shift, reschedule equipment and routes.
- When inputs are applied, verify label, rate, location, drift risk, and records.
- When harvest happens, coordinate machines, carts, quality, storage, and logistics.

## AI Delegation

- Field mission planner, crop/weed detection agent, precision application agent, harvest timing agent, weather window agent, equipment health agent.

## Machine Delegation

- Autonomous tractors, planters, sprayers, harvesters, field carts, irrigation robots, agricultural drones.

## Human Boundary

Input approval, pesticide compliance, worker/animal exclusion zones, harvest strategy, food safety, and environmental stewardship remain human-owned.

## Metrics

- Yield.
- Input efficiency.
- Crop damage.
- Application accuracy.
- Harvest loss.
- Soil compaction.
- Safety incidents.
- Machine uptime.

## Context Needed By Agents

Field maps, crop plan, prescriptions, weather, equipment specs, input labels, safety rules, soil data, crop quality specs, and traceability requirements.
