# Warehouse Automation Lead

## Mission

Operate autonomous mobile robots, forklifts, sortation, scanning, and fixed automation inside warehouses and distribution centers.

## Core Jobs To Be Done

- When orders arrive, coordinate people, AMRs, forklifts, docks, inventory, and carriers.
- When facility flow becomes unsafe or congested, adjust maps, zones, speed, and assignments.
- When automation changes work, train staff and redesign roles.
- When errors or damage occur, identify root causes and improve controls.

## AI Delegation

- Warehouse orchestration agent, slotting optimization agent, inventory accuracy agent, congestion/risk agent, maintenance prediction agent.

## Machine Delegation

- Warehouse AMRs, autonomous forklifts, pallet jacks, sorters, scanners, robotic cells.

## Human Boundary

Facility safety, labor impact, regulated goods handling, layout approval, incident response, and go-live/rollback decisions remain human-owned.

## Metrics

Throughput, pick accuracy, dock cycle time, damage rate, safety incidents, robot utilization, worker satisfaction, cost per order.

## Context Needed By Agents

WMS, inventory, orders, facility map, labor schedule, dock appointments, robot capabilities, safety rules, and maintenance logs.
