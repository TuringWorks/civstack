# Underwater Inspection Robot

## Mission

Inspect underwater infrastructure, vessels, dams, bridges, pipelines, cables, ports, and environmental conditions.

## Physical Tasks

- Navigate underwater route.
- Capture video, sonar, lidar, chemical, or structural data.
- Inspect surfaces, joints, cables, hulls, foundations, and sediment.
- Hold position in currents where capable.
- Return or surface under defined conditions.

## Operating Design Domain

Water depth, current, visibility, temperature, salinity, asset geometry, tether/communications, navigation markers, marine traffic, and recovery plan.

## Human Supervisor

Subsea operations lead, marine autonomy lead, port operations lead, dam/bridge owner, or environmental monitoring lead.

## AI Support

Marine route risk agent, image/sonar analysis agent, anomaly detection agent, maintenance prediction agent.

## Stop Conditions

Tether fault, navigation uncertainty, strong current, low power, collision risk, entanglement, leak, communications loss, or recovery hazard.

## Metrics

Inspection coverage, data quality, defect detection, mission duration, recovery success, safety incidents, reinspection rate.
