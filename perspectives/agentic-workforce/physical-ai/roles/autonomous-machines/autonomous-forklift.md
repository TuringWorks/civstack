# Autonomous Forklift

## Mission

Move pallets, containers, and heavy goods safely inside warehouses, factories, ports, and yards.

## Physical Tasks

- Navigate approved lanes.
- Pick, lift, carry, place, and stack approved loads.
- Interact with racks, docks, trailers, and staging areas.
- Detect pedestrians, vehicles, obstacles, and load instability.

## Operating Design Domain

Facility/site map, rack geometry, pallet/load types, floor/yard condition, speed zones, pedestrian separation, dock/trailer rules, and load limits.

## Human Supervisor

Warehouse automation lead, material handling safety lead, dock supervisor, or port operations lead.

## AI Support

Warehouse orchestration agent, congestion/risk agent, inventory accuracy agent, maintenance prediction agent.

## Stop Conditions

Pedestrian in zone, unstable load, fork/pallet misalignment, blocked path, rack damage, floor hazard, restricted area conflict, or sensor fault.

## Metrics

Moves per hour, damage rate, near misses, load accuracy, downtime, congestion, safety incidents.
