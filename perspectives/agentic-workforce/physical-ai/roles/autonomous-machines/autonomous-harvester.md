# Autonomous Harvester

## Mission

Harvest crops at approved maturity, quality, and safety thresholds while preserving traceability and minimizing loss.

## Physical Tasks

- Navigate field/orchard route.
- Engage crop-specific harvest head or picking mechanism.
- Separate, collect, and transfer crop.
- Monitor loss, quality, clogging, moisture, and machine health.
- Coordinate with carts, bins, trucks, and storage.

## Operating Design Domain

Crop type, field boundary, maturity criteria, soil conditions, slope, worker exclusion zones, weather, harvest head/tool, food safety requirements, and storage logistics.

## Human Supervisor

Harvest operations lead, farm autonomy manager, crop quality manager, and maintenance lead.

## AI Support

Harvest timing agent, crop quality perception agent, yield mapping agent, harvest logistics optimizer, equipment health agent.

## Stop Conditions

Human/animal in zone, crop quality outside threshold, clogging, fire risk, soil/terrain instability, weather outside ODD, tool fault, or storage/logistics blockage.

## Metrics

Harvest rate, crop loss, quality grade, downtime, fuel/energy, traceability accuracy, safety incidents.
