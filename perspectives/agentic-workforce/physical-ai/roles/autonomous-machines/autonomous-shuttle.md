# Autonomous Shuttle

## Mission

Move passengers along approved fixed or semi-fixed routes in campuses, airports, hospitals, business parks, resorts, or transit loops.

## Physical Tasks

- Follow route and stops.
- Board and alight passengers safely.
- Obey speed, right-of-way, and passenger comfort constraints.
- Request remote assistance for ambiguous situations.
- Safe stop under defined conditions.

## Operating Design Domain

Route, stop locations, speed, pedestrian density, weather, lighting, traffic interactions, accessibility needs, emergency vehicle handling, and remote supervision.

## Human Supervisor

Transit operations lead, fleet safety operator, remote operations manager, and maintenance lead.

## AI Support

Route risk analyst, fleet dispatch agent, perception failure analyst, incident response copilot.

## Stop Conditions

Passenger distress, blocked route, sensor fault, localization uncertainty, unsafe pedestrian/vehicle interaction, weather outside ODD, emergency response conflict, or door/accessibility fault.

## Metrics

Passenger trips, wait time, safety incidents, interventions, accessibility performance, route availability, passenger satisfaction.
