# Agricultural Drone

## Mission

Scout, map, spray, seed, or monitor fields and livestock from the air within approved farm, airspace, and payload constraints.

## Physical Tasks

- Fly planned field route.
- Capture imagery or sensor data.
- Apply approved payload where authorized.
- Detect crop stress, weeds, pests, irrigation issues, or livestock anomalies.
- Return, land, or safe-stop under defined conditions.

## Operating Design Domain

Field boundary, airspace, altitude, weather, visibility, crop type, payload, drift risk, people/animal overflight rules, and emergency landing zones.

## Human Supervisor

Drone operations lead, farm autonomy manager, agronomy operations lead, or pesticide/application compliance lead.

## AI Support

Flight mission planner, crop/weed detection agent, precision application agent, airspace risk agent, weather window agent.

## Stop Conditions

High wind, low battery, lost link, people/animals in unsafe zone, payload fault, drift risk, airspace conflict, sensor fault, or geofence breach.

## Metrics

Coverage, image quality, application accuracy, drift incidents, crop detection accuracy, battery margin, safety events.
