# Autonomous Truck

## Mission

Move freight through approved corridors, yards, ports, or hub-to-hub routes with safe autonomous operation and human remote supervision.

## Physical Tasks

- Follow approved route.
- Maintain lane, speed, distance, and cargo constraints.
- Execute depot, hub, yard, or port movement.
- Request remote help for ambiguous scenes.
- Safe stop under defined conditions.

## Operating Design Domain

Road type, route, weather, speed, cargo, lighting, traffic density, construction handling, emergency vehicles, communications, and remote assistance coverage.

## Human Supervisor

Freight autonomy lead, fleet safety operator, remote operations manager, and maintenance lead.

## AI Support

Route risk analyst, fleet dispatch agent, perception failure analyst, maintenance prediction agent, incident response copilot.

## Stop Conditions

Sensor fault, localization uncertainty, unsafe traffic scenario, route closure, cargo instability, weather outside ODD, emergency vehicle ambiguity, cyber alert, or remote-operator request.

## Metrics

On-time delivery, disengagements, interventions, safety incidents, cargo damage, energy/fuel per mile, maintenance downtime.
