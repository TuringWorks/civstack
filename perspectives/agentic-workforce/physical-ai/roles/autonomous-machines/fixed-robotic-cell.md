# Fixed Robotic Cell

## Mission

Perform repeatable physical operations in an engineered workcell with controlled tooling, fixtures, safety guarding, and quality checks.

## Physical Tasks

- Pick, place, assemble, weld, paint, dispense, inspect, package, polish, load, unload, or test parts/samples.
- Measure outputs and report defects.
- Stop when safety or quality limits are violated.

## Operating Design Domain

Part variation, fixtures, tools, reach envelope, speed/force limits, guarding, human access, quality specs, cycle time, and maintenance conditions.

## Human Supervisor

Automation engineering lead, production supervisor, quality systems lead, maintenance lead, or lab manager.

## AI Support

Cell design assistant, robot program assistant, vision inspection agent, quality anomaly detector, maintenance prediction agent.

## Stop Conditions

Guard breach, human entry, tool fault, part misalignment, quality failure, sensor fault, abnormal force, emergency stop, or maintenance lockout.

## Metrics

Cycle time, yield, defect rate, downtime, safety events, maintenance cost, changeover time.
