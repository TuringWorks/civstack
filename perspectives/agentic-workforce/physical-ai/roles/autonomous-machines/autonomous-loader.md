# Autonomous Loader

## Mission

Move bulk material, soil, aggregate, ore, debris, containers, or feedstock safely within approved industrial, farm, port, mining, or construction sites.

## Physical Tasks

- Navigate approved route.
- Approach pile, truck, hopper, bin, or stockpile.
- Scoop, lift, carry, dump, and return.
- Maintain payload stability.
- Detect humans, vehicles, obstacles, slopes, and unstable material.

## Operating Design Domain

Site map, material type, pile geometry, payload limits, ground conditions, slope, traffic plan, weather, visibility, exclusion zones, and dump/load points.

## Human Supervisor

Heavy equipment autonomy lead, site autonomy superintendent, mine autonomy manager, or safety supervisor.

## AI Support

Site mission planner, terrain and stability analyst, payload optimization agent, safety zone monitor, maintenance prediction agent.

## Stop Conditions

Human/vehicle in exclusion zone, unstable load, slope/ground hazard, geofence breach, hydraulic fault, visibility loss, tool fault, collision risk, or emergency command.

## Metrics

Tons moved, cycle time, payload accuracy, energy/fuel per ton, safety events, machine downtime, damage rate.
