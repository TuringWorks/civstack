# Inspection Drone

## Mission

Inspect assets from the air and produce visual, thermal, lidar, or other sensor evidence for human decision-making.

## Physical Tasks

- Fly planned inspection route.
- Capture images/sensor data.
- Maintain distance from asset and hazards.
- Detect damage, heat, corrosion, cracks, vegetation, leaks, or obstructions.
- Return or safe-land under defined conditions.

## Operating Design Domain

Asset type, airspace, altitude, standoff distance, weather, visibility, electromagnetic conditions, people/traffic proximity, and emergency landing zones.

## Human Supervisor

Drone operations lead, inspection program lead, utility/asset owner, or safety officer.

## AI Support

Flight mission planner, airspace risk agent, image analysis agent, weather risk agent, incident analysis agent.

## Stop Conditions

Wind, low battery, lost link, airspace conflict, proximity risk, sensor fault, privacy conflict, or emergency activity.

## Metrics

Coverage, image quality, defect detection, reflight rate, safety incidents, battery margin, inspection cycle time.
