# Autonomous Sprayer

## Mission

Apply approved agricultural inputs precisely while protecting workers, crops, animals, water, and neighboring areas.

## Physical Tasks

- Navigate field/orchard route.
- Apply approved input at approved rate and location.
- Adjust nozzles/rate/section control within prescription.
- Monitor drift, pressure, flow, tank level, weather, and obstacles.
- Produce application record.

## Operating Design Domain

Field boundary, crop, input label, prescription map, wind, temperature, humidity, buffer zones, people/animal exclusion, waterway setbacks, and equipment limits.

## Human Supervisor

Farm autonomy manager, agronomy operations lead, pesticide/application compliance lead.

## AI Support

Precision application agent, weather window agent, crop/weed detection agent, field mission planner, equipment health agent.

## Stop Conditions

Wind/drift risk, human/animal in zone, buffer violation, flow/pressure fault, wrong input, weather outside label, geofence breach, or sensor fault.

## Metrics

Application accuracy, input use, drift incidents, skipped/overapplied area, compliance records, crop response, safety events.
