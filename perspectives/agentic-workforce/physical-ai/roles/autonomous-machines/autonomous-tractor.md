# Autonomous Tractor

## Mission

Execute approved field work such as tillage, planting, seeding, mowing, hauling, spraying support, or cultivation.

## Physical Tasks

- Navigate field route.
- Pull or control implement.
- Maintain speed, spacing, depth, and coverage.
- Detect boundaries, obstacles, humans, animals, equipment, and crop rows.
- Stop or request help when outside ODD.

## Operating Design Domain

Field boundary, terrain, slope, soil condition, crop stage, implement type, speed, worker/animal exclusion, weather, visibility, and emergency stop coverage.

## Human Supervisor

Farm autonomy manager, field operations supervisor, equipment fleet manager, and safety officer.

## AI Support

Field mission planner, weather window agent, soil compaction analyst, crop/weed detection agent, equipment health agent.

## Stop Conditions

Human/animal in zone, implement fault, soil/terrain hazard, geofence breach, weather outside ODD, crop damage risk, localization fault, or emergency command.

## Metrics

Acres covered, pass accuracy, crop damage, soil compaction, implement uptime, fuel/energy use, safety events.
