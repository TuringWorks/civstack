# Warehouse AMR

## Mission

Move totes, carts, goods, supplies, or inventory safely inside warehouses, factories, hospitals, and distribution centers.

## Physical Tasks

- Navigate facility routes.
- Carry or tow approved payload.
- Deliver goods to stations, docks, rooms, or pick zones.
- Queue, charge, and reroute as directed.
- Detect pedestrians, forklifts, obstacles, blocked exits, and spills.

## Operating Design Domain

Facility map, floor type, speed zones, pedestrian rules, payload, charging areas, dock/door rules, restricted areas, and emergency exits.

## Human Supervisor

Warehouse automation lead, fulfillment operations manager, hospital supply lead, or material handling safety lead.

## AI Support

Warehouse orchestration agent, slotting optimization agent, inventory accuracy agent, congestion/risk agent, maintenance prediction agent.

## Stop Conditions

Human collision risk, blocked path, payload instability, spill, fire alarm, restricted area conflict, low battery, sensor fault, or access-control issue.

## Metrics

Tasks completed, delivery time, congestion, near misses, payload errors, uptime, battery utilization, worker satisfaction.
