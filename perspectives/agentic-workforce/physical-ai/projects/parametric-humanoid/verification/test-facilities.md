# Required Test Facilities and Fixtures

## Component laboratory

- Rigid bench with through-bolt capability.
- Polycarbonate guards and remote E-stop.
- Calibrated load cells, weights, torque reference and temperature logging.
- 48 V-capable current-limited supply and protected distribution.
- Tendon termination fixture and contained failure shield.

## Mobility gantry

- Independently engineered overhead structure and rated fall-arrest device.
- Adjustable robot harness that avoids E-stop and moving joints.
- Level instrumented floor with exclusion barrier.
- Motion capture or independent pose measurement.
- Remote control station with direct visibility and camera recording.

## Stair fixture

- Replaceable steps with adjustable rise/tread and force measurement.
- Edge cases including slight registration errors and low-friction surfaces.
- Fall-arrest geometry valid throughout ascent and descent.

## Ladder fixture

- Ground-fixed ladder with interchangeable 25–35 mm rungs and 250–350 mm spacing.
- Adjustable inclination from 75° to 90°.
- Independent rung-load instrumentation.
- Clearance for tether, robot and emergency access.
- Remote release/fault injection without personnel in the fall envelope.

No stair or ladder testing begins until the facility, tether path, rescue plan, observer roles and stop criteria are reviewed together.
