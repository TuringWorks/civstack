# Hardware-Gated Decisions

These decisions cannot be closed honestly from CAD or calculation alone.

| Gate | Evidence required | Decision unlocked |
|---|---|---|
| G1 Plywood/kerf coupon | Measured thickness, kerf, bearing/shaft/fastener fit | Release J1 cutting files with corrected dimensions |
| G2 Tendon termination | Five specimens per method, slip/failure data, 100 bend cycles | Select termination and operating-force derating |
| G3 J1 proof and endurance | 22 N·m proof, thermal test, 500 then 10,000 cycles | Retain or reject M-class opposing-tendon architecture |
| G4 Actuator bench | Torque-speed-efficiency-temperature map at capstan | Freeze M actuator and reduction |
| G5 J2 high-load rig | H-class proof torque and 10,000 representative cycles | Freeze hip/knee/ankle-pitch architecture |
| G6 Foot/ankle rig | Force distribution, slip, edge contact and ankle response | Freeze foot geometry and force-sensor layout |
| G7 Arm/gripper rig | Object and rung retention under power loss | Freeze hand, wrist and ladder-hook design |
| G8 Battery/power bench | Peak current, precharge, fuse selectivity, thermal and shutdown tests | Freeze battery, BMS and power distribution |
| G9 Suspended lower body | Squat, stepping and protective-stop tests under gantry | Authorize guarded untethered-contact experiments while still fall-arrested |
| G10 Stair fixture | Repeatable ascent/descent with fault injections | Authorize progression to ladder fixture |
| G11 Ladder fixture | Positive grip, three-point contact, power-loss and tether tests | Establish whether ladder climbing remains in scope |

No full-body plywood cutting release should occur before G1–G5 are closed.
