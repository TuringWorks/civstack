# Manufacturing Quality Checklist

## Part inspection

- [ ] Part number, revision, sheet ID and orientation legible.
- [ ] Actual thickness recorded.
- [ ] Exterior profile and critical holes measured against corrected drawing.
- [ ] No unacceptable void, crack, burn-through, delamination or warp.
- [ ] Bearing pockets and shaft clearances match approved coupon result.
- [ ] Tendon surfaces are smooth and free of sharp char or splinters.
- [ ] Fastener edge distances and washer seating surfaces are intact.

## Module inspection

- [ ] Bearings fully seated and mechanically retained.
- [ ] Shaft collars and locknuts installed and witness-marked.
- [ ] Joint rotates through approved range without binding.
- [ ] Hard stops act before tendon/cable/shell contact.
- [ ] Tendon preload and termination marks recorded.
- [ ] Encoder direction, zero and plausibility verified.
- [ ] Wiring strain relief and service loops installed.
- [ ] Guarding installed before powered testing.
- [ ] Module mass and center-of-mass estimate updated.

## Release record

Record inspector, date, configuration hash, drawing revision, material batch, deviations, concessions, photographs and release scope.
