# Drone Fleet Operating Model

Purpose: coordinate aerial robotics for inspection, agriculture, delivery, disaster response, mapping, public safety, and environmental monitoring.

## Mission

Use aerial autonomy to gather data or move small payloads safely within approved airspace and privacy boundaries.

## Human Command Roles

- Drone operations lead.
- Remote pilot/operator.
- Airspace compliance lead.
- Mission planner.
- Payload specialist.
- Data/privacy officer.
- Fleet maintenance lead.

## AI Personnel

- Flight mission planner.
- Airspace risk agent.
- Image analysis agent.
- Fleet scheduling agent.
- Weather risk agent.
- Incident analysis agent.
- Privacy redaction agent.

## Autonomous Machines

- Inspection drone.
- Agricultural scouting drone.
- Spray/seeding drone.
- Delivery drone.
- Disaster response drone.
- Mapping/survey drone.
- Security patrol drone.

## Operating Loop

1. Define mission, airspace, payload, data policy, and operator coverage.
2. Plan route, altitude, geofence, battery, and emergency landing zones.
3. Verify drone, payload, communications, weather, and airspace readiness.
4. Execute mission with monitoring.
5. Analyze imagery/sensor data.
6. Deliver findings, payload confirmation, or emergency mapping.
7. Review incidents, privacy issues, and flight performance.

## Required Artifacts

- Flight plan.
- Airspace authorization.
- Weather limits.
- Payload record.
- Emergency landing plan.
- Privacy/data handling plan.
- Maintenance record.
- Incident log.

## Metrics

- Mission completion.
- Flight safety.
- Data quality.
- Battery margin.
- Airspace compliance.
- Privacy incidents.
- Defect/crop/hazard detection accuracy.
