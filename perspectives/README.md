# Perspectives — alternative viewpoints

CivStack is one way to organize the question *"what work must a society do, and who does
it — human, AI, or robot?"* It is not the only way. This folder holds **parallel,
intact takes on the same problem**, kept whole rather than dissolved into the main
library, so they can be read on their own terms and compared.

Keeping a second viewpoint is deliberate: a single taxonomy hides its own assumptions.
Two independent structures over the same domain make those assumptions visible and give
you a second opinion when you design a role, a sector, or a deployment.

## What's here

- **`agentic-workforce/`** — an earlier, **hand-authored** take on the same idea. Same
  human/AI/robot teaming and a 22-domain backbone, but a different shape:
  - `domains/` — one concise workforce skill per domain (22).
  - `operating-models/` — strategic "operating models" (semiconductor sovereignty,
    energy abundance, bioeconomy, frontier-AI production, …) and a Human-AI-Robot
    command system.
  - `physical-ai/` — a focused non-humanoid physical-AI library (autonomous vehicles,
    farm equipment, drones, AMRs) with its own roles, catalogs, checklists, and a
    use-case coverage matrix.
  - `roles/`, `catalogs/`, `checklists/`, `templates/`, `strategies/` — supporting
    role cards, taxonomies, readiness checklists, and a frontier-technology strategy.

## CivStack vs Agentic-Workforce, in one line each

- **CivStack** (`../skills/`): generated, exhaustive, two-axis (23 sectors × 12 missions),
  deep on the *machinery* — capability/optimization spectrum, deskilling/keep-warm,
  job-description grounding, informal economy, the build/operate stack, and accountability
  controls. Regenerable from one generator.
- **Agentic-Workforce** (here): hand-authored, concise, strategy-forward — strong on the
  *frontier-technology thesis*, the physical-AI taxonomy, and crisp readiness checklists.

Neither supersedes the other. See **[`../docs/viewpoints-comparison.md`](../docs/viewpoints-comparison.md)**
for a full cross-walk that maps each Agentic-Workforce artifact to its CivStack counterpart
and says when to reach for which.
